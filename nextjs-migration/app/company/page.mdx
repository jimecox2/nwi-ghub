# Company Information

Northern Wireless consists of 2 main partners, Jim Cox and Stephane Gauvin. We
also employ 2 part-time experts for configuration and installation. Two
investors assist in partial financing of smaller projects.

Our subcontractor Base Controls assembles our wireless equipment. Founded in
1987, this medium-sized company has over 150 employees and 4 divisions. The
company is a privately owned corporation with revenues of $20M annually, with
branch offices in Ottawa, Montreal, Toronto and Kingston.

The manufacturing division of Base Controls is the Controls Depot:

- CSA-certified control panel manufacturing
- Custom panel design and manufacturing
- Design and CAD drawing support
- Quality manufacturing compliant to worldwide standards
- Burn-in test facility up to 60 °C
- ISO-certified test equipment
- Z299-3 quality control
