# Business Summary

The foundation of Northern Wireless Inc. is based on the success of Atikokan
I.S. ([www.nwon.com](http://www.nwon.com/)). Atikokan I.S. is a four-year-old
ISP that has successfully designed and implemented 802.11 wireless internet
access systems in North Western Ontario and in South Eastern Ontario.

The Atikokan I.S. wireless solution is based upon the supply and integration of
"off-the-shelf" 802.11 equipment — a proven solution, providing up to 11 Mbps
broadband to the end user.
