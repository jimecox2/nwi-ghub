# Wireless Access Point

A sound wireless design often involves a wireless device that is installed
remotely from the internet feed router. It becomes necessary to acquire a
climate-controlled cabinet to house the delicate instrumentation.

Northern Wireless provides a CSA-approved access point in a cabinet as part of a
solution or sold separately.

![Climate-controlled cabinet, fully equipped](/images/CabFull.jpg)

Here we have a climate-controlled cabinet designed for 50 degrees below zero
Celsius. This cabinet contains Cisco and Orinoco/Proxim equipment and power
supplies.

![Cabinet installed at the base of an antenna pole](/images/CabTower.jpg)

The cabinet is usually installed at the base of the antenna pole, as seen in the
example above.
