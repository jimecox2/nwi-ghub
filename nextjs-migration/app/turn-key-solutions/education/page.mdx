# Education

Schools everywhere in Canada need broadband for research, video-conferencing,
and for educating our children in the necessary skills to function in the
current business environment. Today many schools outside of the cities use a
single 56k modem to supply bandwidth to many students.

There are many technology choices for schools to choose from. Let Northern
Wireless take the complexity out of your decision making. With our proven
experience we can guide you in the right direction. Why reinvent the wheel? We
have the experience to set up your school with broadband, integrating your
existing equipment into a broadband wireless solution. We want to share our
experience with you.

We recommend only 802.11b (Wi-Fi) and some 802.11a standard equipment for
outdoor networks. These standards are developed by the IEEE, the same working
groups that implemented Ethernet (802.3). We provide only Wi-Fi-certified
equipment to ensure interoperability across our solution. Wi-Fi-certified
products are the most common and least expensive equipment on the market.
