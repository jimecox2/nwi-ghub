## Exclusions

- **Network Interface Card (NIC).** Computers normally ship with a NIC but not always. NWI can provide one for $75.00 including installation/configuration.
- **Local tower and mast.** NWI supplies a bracket to mount antennas to a mast. (We can quote a rooftop tower or 60-foot ground-based towers.)
- Special ladders, cranes and safety equipment
- Climate controlled rooms and power source
- **ISP services** such as email, web, messaging, etc. NWI can provide these services at $10.00 per month per customer.
- Configuration of client VPN or other secure transfer technologies and data encryption
- **Spares.** Keeping recommended spares on hand will provide for an emergency situation.
- **Emergency backup power** for base station and bandwidth control unit is not part of this system. If the power goes out, it normally goes out for the region. We will provide power outage protection and power conditioning on all backbone equipment.
- **Firewalls** are not part of this quote. Firewalls are normally the responsibility of the end user.
- Traffic and usage data collection is not part of this system.
- **System redundancy** – Designing a 100% redundant network is not practical.

## Support (Details)

- Remote configuration and monitoring is standard with all wireless products in this design. Two months of telephone support and remote administration is included.
- Spares, including power supplies, antennas, radios, connectors and cable, should be purchased.
- Documentation and software from vendor equipment will be provided in a manual.
- Generic program updates are not usually required unless more performance, additional features, and security is required.
- **Training of local engineer/technologist/technician.** One-time local training course, 4 hours. Suggest local person follow the commissioning team during construction and commissioning to learn hands-on. Two months telephone support included to allow local person to administer the network.
