# Target Markets

Our target markets are ISPs, communities and small businesses in Canada where
the current form of internet connectivity is the telephone line or expensive
satellite systems.

Existing and start-up ISPs whose current market demands higher internet access
speeds, where the ISP does not have the in-house expertise to design and
implement such a solution, and where an affordable, quick-deployment last-mile
solution is needed.

[Canadian communities](http://www.broadband.gc.ca/maps/maps_e.asp) are our
biggest market. Industry Canada's Broadband for Rural and Northern Development
Pilot Program has attracted over 220 community applicants who require funding for
their local internet access requirements. This Industry Canada broadband
initiative is part of the Government of Canada's commitment to ensuring
broadband access for all Canadian communities by 2005. Over $100M is allocated
to community-sponsored groups. Many of these groups have no expertise in
implementing such a solution.

We are targeting small businesses such as marinas, car dealerships, hotels and
schools that require "hot spots." Hot spots are becoming more popular as
handheld devices become useful in providing mobile access to internet data.
