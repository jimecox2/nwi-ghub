# Internet Service Providers (ISP)

![ISP solutions](/images/icon_isp.jpg)

If you're an ISP or a "start-up" wireless ISP looking for a total "last mile"
solution to reach your customers via broadband, Northern Wireless products and
solutions are your best choice.

## Overview

As an ISP you need a broadband last-mile solution. You need a system that is
more practical and cost-effective than traditional options such as DSL and
cable. Go broadband wireless access with Northern Wireless.

Broadband wireless access is faster, easier and cheaper to deploy than any
alternate solution. By choosing our solution we can have your customers using
wireless broadband in a matter of days. Your network can be scaled to demand.

There are many technology choices once you have made the decision to go wireless
for your last-mile solution. Let Northern Wireless take the complexity out of
your decision making. With our proven experience we can guide you in the right
direction. Why reinvent the wheel? We have already experienced the growing pains
of going wireless and we want to share that experience with you.

We recommend only 802.11b (Wi-Fi) and some 802.11a standard equipment for
outdoor networks. These standards are developed by the IEEE, the same working
groups that implemented Ethernet (802.3). We provide only Wi-Fi-certified
equipment to ensure interoperability across our solution. Wi-Fi-certified
products are the most common and least expensive equipment on the market.

Some wireless suppliers recommend equipment that operates in a licensed spectrum
that forces the ISP to purchase a license to operate in that frequency. Since
it's not that simple to buy a license, the masses purchase the 802.11a or
802.11b license-free equipment — hence the lower prices.

Many wireless suppliers will sell a solution that requires proprietary equipment
(equipment that is only available from that supplier). The initial solution may
seem fairly priced, but customer-premise expansion is costly and it is difficult
to negotiate lower prices. By choosing Northern Wireless as your supplier you
can feel comfortable that you are not saddled with proprietary equipment. We use
open-standard Wi-Fi equipment only.
