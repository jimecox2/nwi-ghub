# Marinas

![Marina wireless](/images/icon_vert_marina.jpg)

Go wireless with Northern Wireless and provide low-cost access to the internet
for your customers. Ed Huck Marine is one example, where boating customers have
access to the internet while on a weekend cruise. Northern Wireless achieved
this using a satellite dish for internet access.

Marinas need to provide their customers with all the common services of today.
The latest new service is the internet. While out on your boat for a week-long
trip, the internet is a necessary information tool for weather, news and general
communication. Why not implement a wireless network for your boating customers?

![Marina wireless network layout](/images/MarinaLayout.jpg)

Go wireless with Northern Wireless and realize how easy and inexpensive it can
be to be a mini broadband wireless ISP.
