# Office Solutions

Today every office needs a broadband solution to do business. The office
environment is often reliant on connectivity to the main office via the
internet. The cost of a wide area network is incredibly high. Your office may be
located a short distance from a local wired internet feed, but the cost to run a
wire to your office is not in the budget — this is where Northern Wireless can
help.

There are many technology choices for you to choose from. Let Northern Wireless
take the complexity out of your decision making. With our proven experience we
can guide you in the right direction. Why reinvent the wheel? We have the
experience to set up your office with broadband, integrating your existing
equipment into a broadband wireless solution. We want to share our experience
with you.

We recommend only 802.11b (Wi-Fi) and some 802.11a standard equipment for
outdoor networks. These standards are developed by the IEEE, the same working
groups that implemented Ethernet (802.3). We provide only Wi-Fi-certified
equipment to ensure interoperability across our solution. Wi-Fi-certified
products are the most common and least expensive equipment on the market.
