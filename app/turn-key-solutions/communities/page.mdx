# Communities

Broadband service is taken for granted in most cities in Canada. In small remote
communities, the internet is a vital step to economic development and
sustainability.

You are a community and you need broadband. Northern Wireless offers a complete
package or partial package solution for you based on 802.11b technology. Your
end-user hardware costs are now down to as low as $215.00. Depending on the
distance and location of your end users, the maximum cost is around $400.00.
