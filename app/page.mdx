# Welcome to Northern Wireless Inc.

We are a newly formed company, a spin-off of Atikokan Information Solutions, a
four-year-old ISP business in North Western Ontario. We bring 12 years of
combined ISP and Wireless ISP experience to you.

Our primary focus is to provide complete wireless ISP solutions and products to
small communities who need broadband, and to small businesses who need
broadband but cannot afford the high prices of the telecommunication companies.
